Rules & Safety Policy

Please read before visiting — keeping Wonder World safe and fun for everyone.

🧦

Grip Socks Required

Non-slip socks must be worn on all play equipment at all times. No shoes in the playground.

👨‍👩‍👧

Parent Supervision Required

Children must be supervised by a parent or legal guardian at all times. Our staff assist but are not responsible for child supervision.

⚠️

Play at Your Own Risk

Use of playground equipment involves inherent risks. By entering, all guests acknowledge and accept these risks.

🚫

No Outside Food or Drinks

For safety and cleanliness of our play area. Water bottles are permitted.

🏗️

No Climbing Exterior Structures

No rough play or pushing. No misuse of equipment. Follow all staff instructions at all times.

📷

Photography & CCTV

Photos may be taken for promotional use. CCTV is in operation. Inform staff on entry if you wish to opt out.

⚖️ Terms of Entry & Liability Waiver

1. Play at Your Own Risk — Use of the playground involves inherent risks including slips, falls, collisions, and injuries. By entering, all guests acknowledge and accept these risks.
2. Parent / Guardian Supervision — Children must be supervised at all times. Our staff are present to assist but are not responsible for child supervision.
3. Health & Safety Rules — Grip socks must be worn. No rough play or pushing. No climbing on exterior structures. Do not use equipment when unwell or injured.
4. Liability Disclaimer — To the fullest extent permitted under New Zealand law, Wonder World, its owners, employees, and contractors are not liable for injuries, accidents, or loss of personal belongings.
5. Photography & Media — Photos or videos may be taken for promotional purposes. Inform staff upon entry if you do not wish to be included.
6. Right of Refusal — Management reserves the right to refuse entry to anyone behaving in an unsafe or inappropriate manner.
7. Cancellations & Refunds — 14+ days before: full refund · 7–13 days before: 50% refund · Under 7 days: non-refundable.
